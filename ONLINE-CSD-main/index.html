Synopsis

The Cabinet Committee on Security approved several major defence deals worth over Rs 92,000 crore to enhance India's combat capabilities and promote self-reliance in defence manufacturing. These include the acquisition of BrahMos missiles and upgrades for Su-30MKI fighter jets. The investments aim to strengthen India's defence infrastructure and partnerships, particularly in missile technology. These developments align with India's strategy to bolster national security and become a key player in the global defence market.
